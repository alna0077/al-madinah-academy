Recitation Path

From sounds to confident recitation.

Many learners love the Quran but struggle with pronunciation, similar sounds, vowels, stopping, and fluency. This path turns correction into calm, repeatable progress.

✓ Makharij and common pronunciation corrections
✓ Harakat, sukoon, madd, tanween, and shaddah practice
✓ Short surahs and gradual Tajweed foundations
