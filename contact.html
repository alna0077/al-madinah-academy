Common Questions

Helpful before enrollment.

Can beginners join?

Yes. Beginners can start with Arabic letters, sounds, early reading, or basic recitation depending on age and goals.

Do you teach adults?

Yes. Adults and non-Arabic speakers can request recitation correction, Arabic foundations, or private support.

How is placement decided?

Placement depends on current reading level, recitation needs, schedule, teacher availability, and the best fit for steady progress.
